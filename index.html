Visit Da Nang

Da Nang is a great place to relax and enjoy the beach, or to explore the many attractions of central Vietnam.

Top three activities to do in Da Nang

Bana Hill

Ba Na Hills is a French-inspired mountain resort in Da Nang, Vietnam, with a cable car system and amusement park.

Dragon Bridge

The Dragon Bridge is a 666-meter long, 37.5-meter wide dragon-shaped bridge in Da Nang, Vietnam, that breathes fire and water at night.

Marble mountains

The mountains are a popular tourist destination and are known for their caves, temples, and pagodas.
